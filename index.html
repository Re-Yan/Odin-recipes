Bicol Express Recipe

Description

Bicol Express, known natively in Bikol as sinilihan, is a popular Filipino dish which was popularized in the district of Malate, Manila but made in traditional Bicolano style.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Gata (Coconut Creme)
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Pork belly (diced)
  • String Beans
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Knorr Pork Cubes

STEPS:

  1. Sautee the onions and garlic
  2. Then add in the pork and let it simmer until all the juices dry out
  3. add in 3/4 liters of water and adjust the flame to low heat
  4. add in the peeled ginger and let it boil for about an hour
  5. Now that most of the water has evaporated, take out the ginger and mix in the Knorr pork cubes
  6. Add the coconut creme and a pinch of salt and let it cook for 5 minutes wile continously stirring
  7. Lastly, add in the string beans
  8. Once the string beans are soft and the consistency of the coconut creme is thick, put out the flames and serve on a plate
  9. Enjoy!
